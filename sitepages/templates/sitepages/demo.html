Come see the future!

In today's business landscape holding the attention of a consumer is a high priority.
However, this fact can still get overlooked when a business promotes itself online, or it gets executed poorly e.g. incessant pop-ups. I suggest a unique alternative or perhaps addition. A simple animation.
When executed well, an animation can be eye-catching, appealing and even elicit a desired emotion! All these factors lead to the consumer being entertained and thus holding their attention while increasing engagement.
Is that it? Not by a longshot.
I have the skills to make an animation unique to a business, place in the forefront or background of the site, and make it interactive! Interactivity can result in even more engagement...How? By making the interactivity interesting and fun.
